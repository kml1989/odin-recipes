This is a Lasagna

Lasagna is a cheesy, saucy, layered pasta party! With meat, melty cheese, and rich tomato or béchamel sauce, it’s baked to golden perfection and guaranteed to warm your soul.

Ingredients

  • Pomodoro (tomato sauce)
  • Pasta sheets
  • Ground beef or pork
  • Ricotta or béchamel sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Salt & pepper
  • Fresh basil (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Cook the pasta sheets until al dente, then set aside.
  3. In a pan, sauté garlic in olive oil, add ground meat, and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in the pomodoro sauce and let it simmer for 10–15 minutes.
  5. In a baking dish, spread a layer of meat sauce, then pasta sheets, then cheese (ricotta or béchamel), and repeat.
  6. Top with mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  7. Bake for about 30–40 minutes, until golden and bubbling.
  8. Let it rest for 10 minutes before serving. Buon appetito!
