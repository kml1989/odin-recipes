This is a Carbonara

Carbonara is creamy, cheesy, salty perfectionâ€”no cream needed, just eggs, cheese, and pancetta magic!

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti (or your pasta of choice)
  • Egg yolks
  • Pecorino Romano cheese
  • Pancetta or guanciale
  • Black pepper
  • Salt (for pasta water)

Steps

  1. Boil pasta in salted water until al dente.
  2. In a bowl, whisk egg yolks and grated Pecorino.
  3. Cook pancetta in a pan until crispy.
  4. Reserve some pasta water and drain the pasta.
  5. Add hot pasta to the pan with pancetta (heat off).
  6. Quickly mix in egg-cheese mixture, adding pasta water as needed for creaminess.
  7. Top with black pepper and more Pecorino. Serve immediately!
