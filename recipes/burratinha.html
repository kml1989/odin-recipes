This is a Burratinha

Creamy on the inside, delicate on the outside—burrata is cheese that dreams are made of. Just slice, drizzle, and devour!

Ingredients

  • Burrata cheese
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Fresh basil
  • Olive oil
  • Balsamic glaze (optional)
  • Salt & pepper
  • Crusty bread (for scooping!)

Steps

  1. Place burrata in the center of a plate.
  2. Slice cherry tomatoes and arrange them around the cheese.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic glaze.
  4. Sprinkle with salt, pepper, and fresh basil.
  5. Serve with warm bread—and dive in!
